One Piece Main Characters

Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates and the main protagonist of the series. He aims to become the Pirate King by finding the legendary treasure known as One Piece.

Roronoa Zoro

Roronoa Zoro is the swordsman of the Straw Hat Pirates and Luffy's first crewmate. He dreams of becoming the greatest swordsman in the world.

Nami

Nami is the navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates and Luffy's second crewmate. She dreams of creating a map of the world, she also likes money A LOT.
